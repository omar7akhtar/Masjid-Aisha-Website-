Frequently Asked Questions

FAQ

Quick answers for prayer times, parking, entrances, daily prayers, and donations.

What time is Jumu'ah?

Jumu'ah prayer begins at 1:00 PM every Friday.

Where should I park?

Parking is available in the front of the building, with additional parking located behind the building.

Which entrance should I use?

Women should use Door 430. Men should use Door 432.

Is the masjid open for daily prayers?

Yes. Please refer to the prayer schedule for daily prayer and iqamah times.

How can I support the masjid?

Donations can be made through the online Mohid platform, by dropping cash or checks into the donation boxes located in the masjid, or by contacting Br. Asad Shahzad regarding specific donation opportunities.
